Who I Am

You had me at Hello World. My name is Callie Tomlinson, I am a Front-End Developer with a full-time devotion to learning and living. My tech journey started out as an assistant web designer for a small digital marketing firm. In working with our developers, I grew tired of struggling to communicate my own ideas, and decided it was time to become a developer myself. Since my initial realization, I have never looked back. My passion for programming is intensified every time I encounter a new technology. In programming, you can never be a master, there is always room to improve and always room to learn. With a growth mindset, the challenge of learning becomes the reward.

What I Do

I am a motivated front-end engineer with some solid experience, and I’m ready to take my skills to the next level. I persistently keep up with new web technologies and I’ve got proficient knowledge in web development. I can work independently without a hitch, but also thrive in a collaborative, team-oriented environment. I’ll bring my passion for writing efficient, maintainable, and reusable code – plus my unwavering commitment to quality and intuitive web interface development. And of course, I’m eager to learn even more. I will always strive to continuously be challenged and mentored so that I can ensure growth as a developer and maintain an ability to learn any language quickly. I have an extreme passion for learning and working with others to improve myself, the team, the product, and the company. Specialties: JavaScript, HTML, CSS, jQuery, AJAX and Ruby on Rails.

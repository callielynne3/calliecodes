Who I Am

My name is Callie Tomlinson, I am a Front-End Developer with a full-time devotion to learning and living. My tech journey started out as an assistant web designer for a small digital marketing/web design firm. In working with our developers, I grew tired of struggling to communicate my own ideas, and decided it was time to become a developer myself. Since my initial realization, I have never looked back.
